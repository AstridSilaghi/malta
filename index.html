Welcome to Malta! Settle in and explore your surroundings.

  • St Paul's Cathedral
  • Palazzo Falson
  • The Mdina Dungeons Museum
  • Mdina Old City Fortress

Day 1 - Madina & Rabat

Discover the historic charm of Mdina and Rabat.

Tip: Visit St. Paul's Catacombs for a touch of history.

Day 2 - Visit the Surroundings & Relax

Explore nearby attractions and enjoy a relaxed day.

Tip: Take a leisurely walk along the Dingli Cliffs.

Day 3 - Valetta & Three Cities

Immerse yourself in the rich history of Valletta and the Three Cities.

Tip: Don't miss the panoramic views from Upper Barrakka Gardens.

Day 4 - Relax, baby

Take a day to unwind and indulge in leisure activities.

Tip: Consider a spa day for complete relaxation.

Day 5 - Gozo

Take a trip to the beautiful island of Gozo.

Tip: Visit the Citadel and enjoy local Gozitan delicacies.

Day 6 - Beach day (Blue Grotto/Boat Cruise)

Enjoy a day at the beach and explore by boat.

Tip: Capture photos of the stunning Blue Grotto.

Day 7 - See you next time, Malta!

Bid farewell to Malta and cherish your memories.

Tip: Plan your return trip to explore more.
